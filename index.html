Sirena
.

I am a lead interdisciplinary designer, creative human, animal lover, and obsessive journal collector. I aspire for a world where humans live amongst cities of nature and urban biodiversity and ecosystems thrive.

Academically, I come from an Architecture, Studio Arts, and Art History background. I am forever absorbing and exploring the world of design. This has lead my work to span across different fields including architecture, interiors, graphic design, exhibition design, information visualisation, and digital illustration.

In 2014, I co-founded Twig Collaborative, an interdisciplinary design studio engaged in a praxis that explores how design can bridge across different fields, peoples, and species. When I’m not fullfilling my duties as lead designer, you’ll likely find me working on illustrations inspired by my day to day life and thoughts (prints of which are sold on Dar Twig).
